Importance of Islamic Names

Choosing an Islamic name for your child is not just a cultural practice but a religious obligation in Islam. The Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) emphasized the importance of good names and even changed names that had bad or inappropriate meanings. Islamic names typically have beautiful meanings derived from Arabic language, often reflecting the attributes of Allah, names of prophets, or positive qualities. For example, "Amina" means "trustworthy", "Zainab" means "fragrant flower", and "Yusuf" means "God increases in piety, power and influence".
